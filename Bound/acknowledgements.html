Bound

A Game of Oaths & Loopholes

For those who see the world as a contract, and always work to improve the terms for everyone.

Acknowledgements

This game exists because other people made games first, and I played them, and they changed how I think about stories.

Powered by the Apocalypse showed me that rules can be a conversation, not a referee. Candela Obscura reminded me that horror works best when you care about the people at the table. And every kid who ever played pretend in a backyard and said "okay but you can't just be invincible, there have to be rules" — you were game designers. You still are.

Bound is built on all of that.
